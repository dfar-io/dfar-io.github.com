## Rules

1. Perma-death (do not place a bedroll)
2. Insane difficulty
3. BM Frequency 1
4. Nightmare speed
5. Feral sense all
6. AI Block 300 (both normal and blood moon)
7. Loot respawn disabled
8. Delete all on death
9. BM Count 64
