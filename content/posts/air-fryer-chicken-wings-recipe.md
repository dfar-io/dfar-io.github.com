## Ingredients

* Olive oil
* Salt & Pepper
* Old Bay Seasoning
* 1 lb thawed chicken wings

Cooks ~8 chicken wings

## Process

1. Preheat Air Fryer to 400F
1. Pat wings dry
1. Toss Wings in a bowl with oil, salt, pepper, and Old Bay.
1. Cook for 7 min
1. Turn and cook another 7 min
