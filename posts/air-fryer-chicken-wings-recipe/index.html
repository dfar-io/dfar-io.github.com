Air Fryer Chicken Wings Recipe

February 27, 2021

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • 1 lb thawed chicken wings

Cooks ~8 chicken wings

Process

  1. Preheat Air Fryer to 400F
  2. Pat wings dry
  3. Toss Wings in a bowl with oil, salt, pepper, and Old Bay.
  4. Cook for 7 min
  5. Turn and cook another 7 min
