Chocolate Strawberries Recipe

April 25, 2021

Needed

  • Chocolate Baking Chips
  • Strawberries
  • Pot and bowl (for double boiler)
  • Wax paper
  • Baking tray

Procedure

  1. Start boiling 1 inch of water in pot.
  2. Dry strawberries, place on paper towel.
  3. Setup bowl with 2/3 chocolate chips.
  4. Setup baking tray with wax paper.
  5. Once boiling, lower heat, place bowl on top.
  6. Stir until melted.
  7. Add 1/3 chocolate chips, stir until melted.
  8. Dip strawberries, place onto wax paper.
  9. Place in fridge for at least an hour.
