Instant Pot Clam Chowder Recipe

March 18, 2022

Ingredients

  • 3x 6.5oz canned clams
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 2 potatoes
  • 2 celery ribs
  • 1/2 package bacon
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 12 cup chicken broth
  • old bay
  • corn starch
  • garlic

Process

  1. Saute on more, add butter
  2. Dice bacon & add to IP, cook to translucent
  3. Dice onion, celery, cook to translucent
  4. Add flour, cook 1 min
  5. Strain clams, add juice & chicken broth, stir
  6. Add diced potates, garlic, stir
  7. Cook manual high 5 minutes, vent
  8. Saute, add clams, heavy whipping cream, old bay
  9. Add 2 tbsp corn starch slurry
