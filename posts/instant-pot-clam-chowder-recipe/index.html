Instant Pot Clam Chowder Recipe

March 18, 2022

Ingredients

  • 2x 6.5oz canned clams
  • 3 slices bacon
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 2/3 cup half & half
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 cup diced potatoes (gold or red)
  • 1 strand thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper

Serves about 2 cups.

Process

  1. Cut bacon into small pieces, saute in IP until almost crispy.
  2. Make 1 cup clam juice/water mixture.
  3. Add butter, onion, celery, thyme, cook until onion translucent.
  4. Add garlic, salt, pepper, stir for 1 minute.
  5. Add potatoes, sugar, clam juice.
  6. Seal IP, 4 minutes manual high pressure, then rest for 3.
  7. Mash potatoes.
  8. Saute (low if possible), add clams and half & half (DO NOT BOIL)
  9. Add corn starch
