Scallops Recipe

May 21, 2021

Ingredients

  • Sea scallops
  • 1 1/2 tsp Old Bay
  • 1/4 cup butter

Process

  1. Preheat grill on high
  2. Melt butter in microwave.
  3. Mix melted butter and Old Bay in bowl.
  4. Dry, than add salt and pepper to scallops.
  5. Add scallops to bowl and toss.
  6. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Flip, cook for 3-4 minutes.
